Mental Health Resources in Gainesville, FL

Hundreds of thousands of people in the US alone are struggling with maintaining their mental health, and knowing where to find helpful resources is the first step. Being a college town, Gainesville, Florida is home to thousands of students, fauclty and staff members, and the stressors that come along with those roles creates a huge demand for mental health providers. There are a plethora of resources, many of them free, that you can look to if you're struggling with mental health. If you or someone you know is struggling, and you live in Gainesville, check out the mental health centers detailed on the map.
